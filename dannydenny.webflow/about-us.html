About Danny & Denny

Danny & Denny Auto Repair has been proudly serving the Bayside, NY community for over a decade. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in auto repair services. We believe in transparency and honesty, ensuring our customers understand every step of the repair process. Our skilled technicians use the latest equipment to provide reliable and efficient service, treating each vehicle as if it were our own.
