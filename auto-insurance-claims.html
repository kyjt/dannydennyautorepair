What to Do After an Accident: A Step-by-Step Guide

Ensure Safety: 
Move to a safe location if possible. Check for injuries and call 911 if needed.  
Exchange Information: 
‍Collect names, contact numbers, and insurance details from all parties involved. Get contact information from any witnesses.
Document the Scene:
Take photos of the damage to all vehicles involved. Note the time, date, and location of the accident.
Notify Your Insurance Company:
Report the accident to your insurance company as soon as possible. Provide them with all the details and documentation.
Contact Danny & Denny Auto Repair:
‍Call us at 718-229-5091 to schedule an appointment. We handle insurance claims and repairs, making the process smooth and hassle-free.
Bring Your Vehicle for Inspection: 
‍Visit us at 21508 42nd Ave, Bayside, NY for a comprehensive inspection. We will provide a free estimate and begin repairs with your approval.
Leave the Rest to Us: 
‍We work directly with your insurance company to manage the claim. Pay any applicable deductibles, and we’ll take care of the rest.
